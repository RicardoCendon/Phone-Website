Return Policy

  • You can return an item you bought from the Mobile Garage within 15 calendar days after the day you received it.
  • Once you start the return process, you’ll have 14 days to complete the return.
  • Your refund will go to the payment method you used, minus any delivery-related costs, no more than 14 days from when your device is received.
  • Restocking fees don't apply when a device is returned because the item was defective or if the incorrect item was delivered.
  • The following return process also applies to orders delivered in-person or bought during the delivery appointment.

Step 1 - Tell us what you'd like to return

  1. Find the order with the item you want to return.
  2. Under the order you want to return, select View details.
  3. Follow the onscreen instructions. After a few hours, you’ll get a confirmation email. Follow the instructions from the email.

Tip: If you see "Device is not eligible for return" your shipment either hasn’t shipped yet or has passed the 15 day return limit. If it hasn’t shipped yet, you may be able to cancel or change your order.

Step 2 - Prepare your return package

Important: Make sure to transfer, back up, or erase all personal and other data on the device before you send it to Mobile Garage. Mobile Garage isn’t responsible for any use or disclosure of any data that's on the device.

You don’t need to include anything that didn’t come with the device. Make sure to remove the following items from your package:

  • SIM card.
  • Device cases.
  • Extended batteries.
  • Other accessories that didn’t come with the device.

Step 3 - Send your item back to Mobile Garage

  • To be sure the device arrives in its original condition, use the label and instructions from the Mobile Garage email.
  • If you’re returning multiple items, package and ship each item separately with its unique return and shipping label. If you don’t ship each item with its correct label, there may be a delay in processing your return.
  • A refund can take 1–14 business days once the return product is inspected.
